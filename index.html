Internal Ops Request & Triage Engine
n8n · Slack · Google Gemini · Google Sheets
expand ↓
Staff post requests to a Slack channel. Gemini AI reads each one, classifies it, assigns a priority, and logs everything to Google Sheets — no one has to sort or follow up manually.
How it works
  • 01A new message posted to the #ops-requests Slack channel triggers the workflow
  • 02Google Gemini AI reads the message and classifies it into one of five categories (IT, HR, Finance, Facilities, General)

  • 06Every request is logged to Google Sheets with full metadata — timestamp, category, priority, and original message

06
Dental Clinic Appointment Automation
Tally · n8n · Gmail · Google Sheets
expand ↓
Two workflows that handle the full patient appointment lifecycle — instant confirmation, a 24-hour reminder that only fires when it makes sense, and automatic no-show follow-up to both the admin and the patient.
Workflow 1 — Booking & Reminder
  • 01Patient submits a Tally booking form — name, email, date, time, appointment type
  • 02n8n logs the record to a Google Sheets CRM instantly
  • 03A confirmation email goes out to the patient straight away
  • 04An IF node checks whether the appointment is more than 24 hours away
  • 05If yes — a Wait node pauses the workflow until exactly 24 hours before the appointment, then fires a reminder email
  • 06If no (same-day booking) — workflow stops cleanly, no duplicate email sent
Workflow 2 — No-Show Detection
  • 01Admin manually marks a patient as "No-Show" in the Google Sheet Status column
  • 02Google Sheets Trigger detects the row update
  • 03An Edit Fields node reformats date and time values from Sheets serial format to readable text
  • 04An IF node confirms the status is exactly "No-Show"
  • 05Two Gmail nodes fire simultaneously — an alert to the admin with patient details, and a warm rebooking email to the patient
Tools used
Tallyn8nGmailGoogle Sheets
Results
  • →Zero manual effort from admin for confirmations and reminders
  • →Same-day bookings handled correctly — no unnecessary duplicate emails
  • →No-shows trigger follow-up within minutes of the admin updating the sheet
  • →Patients get a warm rebooking email automatically — fewer lost appointments
  • →Entire stack runs on free-tier tools — no ongoing software cost to the clinic
